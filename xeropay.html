What happens after my free trial?

After your 3-day free trial expires, XeroAI will pause AI trading until a Daily Access plan is activated. Your dashboard will remain accessible, but the AI Trading Engine cannot be started until payment has been successfully verified.

Secure Subscription Verification

  • Subscription status is verified directly from the XeroAI server.
  • Clearing browser data, cookies, or cache will not reset the free trial.
  • Signing in on another device will not restart the trial period.
  • Only a successful payment verified by Flutterwave will activate Daily Access.
  • This protects the fairness and security of the XeroAI platform for all users.
