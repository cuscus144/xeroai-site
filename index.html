SECURITY & TRANSPARENCY

Why Traders Trust XeroAI

XeroAI is built on three principles: security, transparency, and control. You always know what's happening with your account — and you're always the one in charge.

Your Funds Stay With Your Broker

XeroAI never holds, moves, or takes custody of your capital. Your money remains in your own broker or exchange account at all times.

Secure API Connections

Every link uses encrypted, permissioned API keys — never your login credentials, and never withdrawal access.

You Control Your Risk

Set your own position sizing, exposure, and drawdown limits. The AI operates strictly inside the boundaries you define.

Transparent Trade History

Every trade, signal, and execution is logged and visible on your dashboard — nothing happens in a black box.

Disconnect Anytime

Revoke API access or pause automation in a single click. No lock-in, no waiting period, no questions asked.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with XeroAI.

XeroAI is an AI-powered trading automation platform. It connects to your existing broker or exchange account, continuously scans the markets, and executes trades automatically when conditions match the strategy and risk settings you configure. XeroAI is not a broker and does not provide trading signals.

Every new account starts with a free 3-day trial with full access to the dashboard and automation features. No credit card is required to begin.

After your trial ends, you continue on a flexible Pay-As-You-Use subscription. There are no fixed monthly fees — you pay only for the automation you actively use.

XeroAI connects to Deriv, HFM, Exness, IC Markets, Pepperstone, and MT5-compatible brokers, as well as exchanges including Binance, Bybit, OKX, Bitget, Gate.io, and KuCoin — with more integrations planned.

API connections use encrypted, permissioned keys that never include withdrawal access or your account login. Keys can be revoked at any time from your dashboard.
